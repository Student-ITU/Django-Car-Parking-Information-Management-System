Problem Solved (Complex Engineering Problem)
Courier operations frequently face critical issues that lead to delays and wasted resources. This system addresses the following key problems:

Inefficient Routing: It eliminates manual route selection, which often leads to higher fuel consumption and delayed deliveries.

Rider-Task Mismatch: It ensures the most suitable rider (nearest and available) is assigned to a task, preventing inconsistent service.

Slow Lookup: It provides fast parcel status checks to quickly retrieve information via tracking IDs, improving customer satisfaction.

Priority Conflicts: It manages the conflicting needs of serving urgent medical parcels first while maintaining overall operational fairness.
